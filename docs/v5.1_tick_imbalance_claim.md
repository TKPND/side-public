This document seals the v5.1 tick imbalance claim before Phase 114 feature
implementation. Downstream phases must consume the constants below literally.
Changing the claim requires a new versioned SEAL; do not mutate this document in
place after the claim-doc-only SEAL commit.

## 2. Source Contract

The source interpretation is `top-of-book quote imbalance proxy`.

No exchange-native L2 order book depth is claimed.
No true aggressor trade flow is claimed.

`bidVolume` and `askVolume` are Dukascopy quote-side volume fields under the
current source contract. They are allowed inputs for a proxy score only. If the
data contract later marks `phase114_blocked: true`, Phase 114 must stop before
signal implementation. If bid/ask volume semantics fail audit, the
`volume_ratio_imbalance` family fails close; the sealed FWER denominator remains
216 and must not be silently shrunk.

## 3. Score Families

| Score family | Formula | Interpretation |
|--------------|---------|----------------|
| `volume_ratio_imbalance` | `volume_ratio_imbalance = (bidVolume - askVolume) / (bidVolume + askVolume)` | top-of-book quote-side volume imbalance proxy |
| `mid_price_tick_direction_imbalance` | `mid_price_tick_direction_imbalance = (up_mid_ticks - down_mid_ticks) / total_mid_ticks` | mid-price tick direction imbalance proxy |

Both score families are sealed now even if one family later fails close. Losing
branches cannot be removed after seeing data.

## 4. Bucket and Entry Semantics

Lookback buckets are exactly `30s`, `60s`, and `300s`.

Each feature bucket contains only ticks strictly before the entry decision. Entry
timing is the next executable tick or bar after the feature bucket closes. Phase
114 must include leakage tests proving future ticks are not used in the entry
feature.

## 5. Hypothesis Family

The sealed family is:

`2 pairs x 2 score families x 3 lookbacks x 3 thresholds x 2 directions x 3 horizons = 216 hypotheses`

| Axis | Sealed values |

| directions | `mean_reversion`, `momentum` |
| horizons | `1m`, `3m`, `5m` |

Mean reversion and momentum are in one multiple-testing family. Buy-side and
sell-side extremes are evaluated symmetrically within each direction rule and do
not justify removing failed branches.

## 6. Split and Cost Model

IS: 2024-05-01 through 2025-10-31
OOS: 2025-11-01 through 2026-04-30

Execution costs are deducted before ship metrics. Spread, slippage, and fees are
primary. Gross PF is diagnostic only.

Primary ship evidence must use net execution-cost-adjusted metrics. Gross PF may
appear in tables only as a diagnostic sanity check and cannot support a ship
claim.

## 7. Missing-Data and Event-Count Gate

Cells fail close when required entry or exit anchors are missing. Pair/month row
counts, timestamp range, null rates, spread sanity, volume null/zero rates, and
missing bucket counts come from `reports/v5.1/tick_data_contract_report.json`.

Sparse cells fail close under the event-count/Nyquist gate selected in Phase 115.
Phase 115 may report sparse-cell counts, but it may not shrink the sealed family
or redefine score axes after seeing sparse results.
